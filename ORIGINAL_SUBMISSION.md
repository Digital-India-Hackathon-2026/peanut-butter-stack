## Idea
VitalGuard: Smart Patient Monitoring & Emergency Response System
Hospitals rely on multiple monitoring systems such as ECG monitors, pulse oximeters (SpO₂), heart rate monitors, nurse call systems, and surveillance cameras to monitor patients. However, these systems operate independently, requiring healthcare professionals to monitor multiple screens while caring for several patients. In ICUs, emergency wards, and general wards, critical warning signs such as repeated requests for help, patient falls, abnormal ECG readings, sudden drops in oxygen saturation, or prolonged inactivity may occur across different systems, making it difficult to quickly identify patients requiring immediate attention.
According to the World Health Organization (WHO), one in every ten hospitalized patients experiences harm during healthcare, contributing to more than three million deaths globally each year due to unsafe care. This highlights the need for a unified patient monitoring and decision-support platform.
VitalGuard is a Smart Patient Monitoring & Emergency Response System that integrates patient audio, video, and physiological monitoring into a centralized software platform. The system detects emergency phrases, identifies the speaker, monitors falls and bed-exit attempts, tracks ECG, heart rate, and SpO₂ data, and intelligently correlates information from multiple monitoring sources to generate a single prioritized alert instead of multiple isolated notifications. A centralized dashboard displays patient status, live vital signs, alert history, and real-time notifications, enabling healthcare professionals to identify critical situations faster while reducing alarm fatigue. By integrating existing hospital monitoring systems into one platform, VitalGuard aims to improve patient safety, support faster emergency response, and enhance the efficiency of hospital monitoring without disrupting existing clinical workflows.
